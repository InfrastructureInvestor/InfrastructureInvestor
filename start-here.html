Guided tracks

Start here, by the seat you sit in

The platform is deep enough to get lost in, thirty-five sub-sector references, thirteen tools, two hundred worked assets. These four tracks sequence it for the reader you actually are, each step building on the last. Work a track top to bottom and you will come out fluent.

Before you start, four habits that make the whole site work harder for you:

Search anythingPress ⌘K (or /) anywhere, it finds pages, worked assets ("Heathrow"), tools, jargon ("towerco") and all 200 glossary terms.
Share a scenarioEvery model has a "Share scenario" button, the link reopens your exact slider settings. Send a colleague your underwrite, not a screenshot.
Stress itEvery calculator has one-click macro scenarios, rates +200bp, an inflation shock, a credit crunch, applied and reversed instantly.
Take it to ExcelEvery projection table has a Download CSV button. The models are transparent, check the maths yourself.
