Model A · merchant

Merchant toll concession

The owner keeps the toll and bears full traffic risk: revenue is volume × price, and rises or falls with the economy and the accuracy of the traffic forecast. The classic private toll-road/bridge model — highest potential return, and the most exposed if the cars don't come.
