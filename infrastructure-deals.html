Infrastructure deals

Landmark infrastructure acquisitions across sectors, regions and decades, the transactions that defined how the asset class trades. Filter by sector, region or deal type, sort any column, and click a target through to its sub-sector reference page.

A companion to the Infrastructure M&A module. Values are at announcement, as reported, a mix of enterprise and equity value, rounded; consortium deals show the whole transaction. Use it as a comparable set: what sells, to whom, how it is structured, and at what scale.

Methodology & sources. Figures are drawn from company announcements, regulatory filings and contemporaneous trade and financial press; full source links are listed in the M&A module references. "Deal value" mixes enterprise and equity value as reported, and several deals later took very different paths (Thames Water's restructuring; the CK Hutchison ports portfolio, agreed 2025, was subsequently contested in Panama).
